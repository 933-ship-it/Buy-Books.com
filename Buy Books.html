Sample Insights

Adopt a Growth Mindset

Dr. Carol Dweck's research shows that viewing intelligence as flexible encourages persistence and learning. Replace "I'm not good at this" with "I'm not good at this yet."

Use Active Recall

Instead of rereading notes, quiz yourself frequently. Retrieval practice is one of the most effective study strategies according to cognitive science.
